How It Works

~ three phases of natural selection ~
01

Drop

Your raw idea enters the ecosystem. We sharpen it — ICP, geography, core problem statement, hypothesis.

→
02

Battle

Animals attack in elimination rounds. Weakest argument dies each round. Survivors absorb the kill insight.

→
03

Emerge

Battle-hardened idea with unfair advantage statement. Or the idea dies — and three pivots are generated.

◈

Three Outcomes

Every battle ends one of three ways.

Survived

Core thesis held

The idea withstood every attack. Patches were cosmetic. Structural advantage confirmed. Build it.

Restructured

Premise died, tech preserved

The original pitch was killed but core technology survived. New ICP, new moat, new angle emerged.

Killed

Pivot engine activates

No structural advantage found. Three alternative pivots generated and mini-battled for survivability.
